A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Error Loading Image Great Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear programs.

About the Legend
  • A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaran, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Kalam earned a degree in aeronautical engineering from the Madras Institute of Technology and in 1958 joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
